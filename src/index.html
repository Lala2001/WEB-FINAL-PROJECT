Ludo is a two to four person strategy board game in which players race their four tokens from beginning to end based on the results of a single die roll. The highest die roll of each player starts the game. In a clockwise rotation, players switch turns. A player must roll a six in order to bring a token from its yard to its starting square into play. If you are the first player to place all of your tokens in the home triangle, you have won the game and can wait for the other players to complete and take second, third, or fourth place.

Ludo is more than just a game. It helps with the development of cognitive abilities like decision-making and problem-solving for both adults and kids. Also, Ludo is a great game for kids to practice counting, colors, and shapes.

Here are some basic information/requirement of Ludo:
  • Playing time: less than 90 minutes
  • Age range: 3+
  • Players: 2-4
  • Materials: Ludo board, 4 tockens of same color(for each player), 1 die
